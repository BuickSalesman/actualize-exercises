Sweet and Sour Ribs

I love them so much<3

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Pork Ribs
  • 2 tbsp AP flour
  • 30 grams garlic
  • 30 grams ginger
  • 30 grams shallot/green onion
  • 2 star anise
  • 4 cloves
  • 6 tbsp yellow rock sugar
  • 4 tbsp black vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp cooking wine
  1. 1 put all ingredients in a pot and boil for 14 hours
  2. serve chilled
